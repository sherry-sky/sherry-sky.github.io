Anode: in a galvanic cell, the anode is the electrode at which the oxidation reaction 
occurs and the electrons leave away. 
Cathode: the electrode that the reduction reaction occurs and the electrons enter. 
Usually, the anode is placed on the left side, and the cathode is placed on the right 
side.
