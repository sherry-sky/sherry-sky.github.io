## 背景知识点

<!-- BACKGROUND_START -->
- **重量分析**：通过称量沉淀的质量来确定被测组分含量的分析方法
- **电双层结构**：沉淀表面带有电荷，周围形成电双层
- **洗涤液选择**：应选择含有与电双层相同离子的电解质溶液，防止胶溶现象
- **成核与生长**：在高度过饱和溶液中，成核作用占主导；在低过饱和溶液中，颗粒生长占主导
- **洗涤原则**：少量多次洗涤，使用含电解质的洗涤液
<!-- BACKGROUND_END -->

## 📋 作业题目

<!-- QUESTION_START id="H12-1" -->
**H12-1.** According to its electric double layer structure, choose the best washing liquid for the precipitate AgCl, which grows in a solution HCl with excess of AgNO₃.

a. H₂O (boiling point: 100 °C)
b. NH₄NO₃ (boiling point: 210 °C)
c. (NH₄)₂CO₃ (boiling point: 336 °C)
d. KNO₃ (boiling point: 400 °C)

**H12-2.** Gravimetric analysis is a fundamental method in chemistry. Indeed, most chemists will be familiar with quantitative analytical gravimetry. Precipitation is exploited in many separation methods, e.g. casein precipitation in food production, potassium hydrogen tartrate deposition in wine, the removal of arsenic by precipitation from waste water. Which statements are appropriate to this method as shown followed:

a. Washing the precipitate with many cycles instead of one time
b. Washing the precipitate by pure water
c. Keeping more volume of precipitate's liquid when doing the filtration
d. In precipitation, nucleation dominates over particle growth in a highly supersaturated solution

<!-- QUESTION_START id="H12-3" -->
**H12-3.** An unknown sample containing only silver nitrate and mercurous nitrate was dissolved in water and treated with excess sodium cobalticyanide, Na₃[Co(CN)₆] to precipitate both cobalticyanide salt:

| 化合物 | 摩尔质量 (FW) |
|--------|--------------|
| AgNO₃ | 169.873 |
| Ag₃[Co(CN)₆] | 538.643 |
| Hg₂(NO₃)₂ | 525.19 |
| (Hg₂)₃[Co(CN)₆]₂ | 1633.62 |

The unknown sample weighed 0.4321 g, and the precipitate product weighed 0.4515 g. Find the weigh percent wt% of AgNO₃ in the unknown sample.
<!-- QUESTION_END -->

---

## ✅ 参考答案

<!-- ANSWER_START for="H12-1" -->
**H12-1.** 洗涤液选择

**答案：b. NH₄NO₃**

**分析：**

首先，应选择含有与目标沉淀电双层相同离子的电解质作为洗涤液。如果使用 H₂O 作为洗涤液，电双层会被稀释，带电颗粒相互排斥，沉淀会分散（胶溶现象）。

根据这个规则，b (NH₄NO₃) 和 d (KNO₃) 都可以选择。

但 NH₄NO₃ (沸点 210 °C) 比 KNO₃ (沸点 400 °C) 更好，因为 NH₄⁺ 在较低温度下容易转化为 NH₃ 挥发，便于后续干燥。

**答案：** **b. NH₄NO₃**
<!-- ANSWER_END -->

<!-- ANSWER_START for="H12-2" -->
**H12-2.** 重量分析陈述判断

**答案：a**

**分析：**
- **a. 正确** - 少量多次洗涤比一次性大量洗涤更有效，可以更彻底地去除杂质
- **b. 错误** - 使用纯水洗涤会导致胶溶现象，应使用含电解质的洗涤液
- **c. 错误** - 过滤时应尽量去除母液，减少杂质
- **d. 正确** - 在高度过饱和溶液中，成核作用占主导，形成大量小颗粒；在低过饱和溶液中，颗粒生长占主导，形成大颗粒

**答案：** **a**
<!-- ANSWER_END -->

<!-- ANSWER_START for="H12-3" -->
**H12-3.** 混合物中 AgNO₃ 含量计算

**已知条件：**
- 样品质量：0.4321 g
- 沉淀质量：0.4515 g

**设：** x g AgNO₃，y g Hg₂(NO₃)₂

**方程组：**
$$x + y = 0.4321\ \text{g}$$

对于沉淀：
$$\frac{x}{169.873} \times \frac{1}{3} \times 538.643 + \frac{y}{525.19} \times \frac{1}{3} \times 1633.62 = 0.4515\ \text{g}$$

**解方程：**
$$1.0568x + 1.0384y = 0.4515$$

代入 y = 0.4321 - x：
$$1.0568x + 1.0384(0.4321 - x) = 0.4515$$
$$1.0568x + 0.4487 - 1.0384x = 0.4515$$
$$0.0184x = 0.0028$$
$$x = 0.1733\ \text{g}$$

**AgNO₃ 质量百分比：**
$$\text{wt\%} = \frac{0.1733\ \text{g}}{0.4321\ \text{g}} \times 100\% = 40.11\%$$

**答案：** AgNO₃ 的质量百分比为 **40.11%**
